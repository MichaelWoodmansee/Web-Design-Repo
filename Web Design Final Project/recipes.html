Chicken Soup

Ingredients

- Chicken Stocks
- Celery
- Carrots
- White onions
- 1 or 2 lB of Chicken
- Dill
- Vegetable Oil
- Parsley
- Scallions

Cooking Instructions

In a large pot, put olive oil chopped onions, let them the onions soften then put the carrot and celery in. once the the vegges have soften put in the chicken stock and the the parsley and the chicken. let the soup cook unitl the the chicken is cooked thought then add the scallions and Dill after a few minutes your soup is ready
